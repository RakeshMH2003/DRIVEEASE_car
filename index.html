Data We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, make a booking, or contact support. This includes your name, email address, phone number, city, and booking history.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate and improve the DriveEase platform — processing bookings, communicating confirmations, resolving disputes, and personalizing your experience. We never sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted storage, secure connections (HTTPS), and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Cookies

DriveEase uses cookies and local storage to maintain your session and preferences. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect platform functionality.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. You can manage your profile from your dashboard or contact our admin team at admin@driveease.com.
